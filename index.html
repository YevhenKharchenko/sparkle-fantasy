Story

In a world consumed by shadow, a mysterious journey begins. You are the chosen bearer of the enchanted orb slinger, destined to seek the five lost keys of Sparkle. With every match, light returns to the realm. Explore enchanted forests, forgotten temples, and lost ruins in a beautifully immersive puzzle adventure.

Features

Orb-Matching Action

Match 3+ orbs to destroy dark chains of magic.

Fantasy World

Journey through over 90 hand-crafted levels.

Powerful Enchantments

Unlock magical upgrades and boosts.

Epic Soundtrack

Original orchestral music enhances the fantasy.

Multiple Game Modes

Adventure, Survival, and Challenge.

No Ads, No Distractions

A premium, immersive experience.

Enchantments

Discover and equip over 20 unique enchantments that alter your powers. Freeze time, summon burning light, or explode darkness in style. Build your strategy as the difficulty grows.

How to Play

  • Use your orb slinger to launch matching colors.

  • Match 3+ orbs to destroy the chain before it reaches the abyss.

  • Unlock and apply enchantments to gain the upper hand.

  • Complete levels, earn stars, and unlock new challenges.
